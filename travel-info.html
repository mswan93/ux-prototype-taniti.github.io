Travel Info for New Visitors

Use this page to plan your arrival, understand local transportation, and learn practical details before your trip.

Getting Here

Getting Here

  • Most visitors arrive by air.
  • A small cruise ship also arrives weekly.
  • The island has a small airport with future expansion planned.
Getting Around

Getting Around

  • Buses serve Taniti City daily.
  • Private buses travel throughout the island.
  • Taxis, rental cars, and bicycles are available.
Money & Communication

Money & Communication

  • Taniti uses the U.S. dollar.
  • Many businesses accept cards and some foreign currencies.
  • English is common in tourist areas.
Safety & Services

Safety & Services

  • One hospital and several clinics serve the island.
  • Petty crime can occur in busy tourist areas.
  • Visitors should plan around local holidays and hours.
